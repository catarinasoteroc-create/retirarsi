The Philosophy

Where rest becomes transformation

Retirarsi — Italian for 'to withdraw, to retreat' — is an invitation to step away from the noise and return to what matters most: your health, your aliveness, your sense of purpose.

Through an integrative approach spanning yoga, movement, somatic therapy, nutrition, herbal medicine and more, each retreat is a deeply personalised journey — held with care, rigour and warmth.

My Story

Who I am

Health is a balance between body, mind, emotions and environment — and returning to it is always possible.

I'm Kate. My path began in nature — in the hills of Serra da Arrábida, where I grew up surrounded by medicinal plants and the living wisdom of my grandparents. From an early age, I understood that health goes far beyond the physical body. It is a balance between body, mind, emotions and environment.

Personal challenges in childhood led me into a profound process of inner transformation — and that experience became the beginning of my search to understand human beings in an integrated way, and to support others on their own path of healing and reconnection.

I studied Naturopathy at the Instituto de Medicina Tradicional in Lisbon, then deepened my knowledge in Ayurveda, Ortho-molecular Nutrition and Psychoneuroimmunology. I trained as a Clinical Trauma Specialist at the Arizona Trauma Institute, and studied Phytotherapy and Manual Therapies at the Escola de Medicina Chinesa da Estefânia. I began my formation in Anthroposophic Medicine at the Arlesheim Academy in Switzerland — an approach that deeply inspires me for the way it integrates the physical, emotional and spiritual in clinical practice. I am currently continuing my studies at the School of Health in London.
